About me

I have always had an interest in programming but my interest peaked when I met one of my friends who was a self taught programmer, seeing him create websites seemed like magic to me. He referred me to moringa school an accelerated coding bootcamp, I spent approximately 1200 hours in moringa where I specialised in full stack web development learning how to make extensive applications using technologies such as html , css, javascript, python with flask and django. One of the applications that I created was Neighbourhood watch, a web application which helps to connect residents of a neighbourhood, showing businesses and news in the neighbourhood. Towards the end I led a team of four in creating a smart irrigation system that automates irrigation.
